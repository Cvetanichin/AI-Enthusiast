A small, understated label for prices, formats, status, or categories. Square corners, brand rose.

```jsx
<Badge>One-time</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline" dot>Instant delivery</Badge>
<Badge variant="charcoal">New</Badge>
```

Variants: `rose` (pale fill, default), `outline`, `charcoal`, `soft` (warm neutral). Keep labels to one or two words.
