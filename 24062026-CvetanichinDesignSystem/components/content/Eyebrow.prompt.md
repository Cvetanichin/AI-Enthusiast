The wide-tracked uppercase label that announces a section — pair it above a serif heading for the brand's signature rhythm.

```jsx
<Eyebrow tone="rose">Who this is for</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="on-dark">What you will have</Eyebrow>
```

Tones: `rose` (default, on light), `rose-light` / `on-dark` (on charcoal), `muted`. Keep the text short — two to four words.
