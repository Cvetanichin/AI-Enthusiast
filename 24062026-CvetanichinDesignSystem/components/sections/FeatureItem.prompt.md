A numbered outcome tile (dark grid) or a ledger-style step row (light), each anchored by a large ghost serif numeral.

```jsx
{/* "What you'll have" — dark tiles in a 2-col grid */}
<FeatureItem number="01" title="A defined offer"
  description="What you're selling, who it's for, and the result — in one sentence." />

{/* "How it works" — light ledger rows */}
<FeatureItem variant="step" number="Section 1" title="Identify what you already have"
  description="A 10-minute audit surfaces sellable knowledge. You end with one offer sentence." />
```

Use `numbered` tiles on a charcoal section (2-column grid, 1px gap); use `step` rows on an ivory section, stacked under a top hairline.
