A calm, italic serif quote for testimonials and key beliefs. The accent variant fills a whole section in rose.

```jsx
<Quote attribution="The First Offer Kit, Section 1">
  People do not pay for experts. They pay for someone who is a few steps ahead of them.
</Quote>
<Quote variant="inset" attribution="Vaska Cvetanoska">You have experience.</Quote>
```

Variants: `accent` (full rose section + ghost mark), `plain` (charcoal on light), `inset` (rose left-rule card). Keep quotes to one or two sentences — let them breathe.
